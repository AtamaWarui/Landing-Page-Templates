Our Story

Nagano is a modern Japanese city and a popular gateway to a variety of sightseeing activities, with Nagano skiing being a major attraction during the winter months. Nagano Japan is also well known for the 1998 Winter Olympics with some of the Nagano ski resorts playing host to many of the events.

Testimonial

Why Ski Nagano?
Nagano is easily accessible from Tokyo, and with the Nagano ski resorts, it doesn’t just need to be all about the skiing and snowboarding. A ski Japan holiday can also provide cultural immersion and an opportunity to partake in some great sightseeing activities around Nagano.

Reviewer 1

Nagano skiing also has the distinct advantage that there are lots of high quality ski resorts within close proximity of each other, so it’s possible to sample multiple Japan ski resorts in the one holiday.

Reviewer 2

Nagano and the surrounding areas offer various activities, many of which highlight the historical richness of Japan. Nagano things to do include a visit to the Zenko-ji Temple or the former castle town of Matsushiro.

Reviewer 3

The nearby town of Matsumoto features the impressive Matsumoto Castle, and a visit to the Japanese snow monkeys and the traditional onsen village of Shibu are quintessential activities whilst on a Nagano skiing holiday.
